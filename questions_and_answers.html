When will the rally take place?

The Rally will beging on August 1st 2022 and will last for five weeks, one week in each country.

Where will the rally take place?

The Rally will be conducted across five countries, starting in South Africa then proceeding to Bostwana then Zambia then Tanzania and will wind up in Malawi

Who can participate?

To qualify you must be of 18 years and above and have a drivers license and no criminal record

Prizes?

The winner will go home with £100,000, second postion will receive £50,000 and third position a vinatge mercedez SLS.
